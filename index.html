Camups Life

Promoted in 1975, Modern School, Vasant Vihar is a co-educational day school affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. Back in 1920, at a time when education was considered to be the privilege of only the rich, Lala Raghubir Singh envisioned a school that would provide exceptional educational opportunities to all students irrespective of caste, creed and social status. His dreams took the shape of the first Modern School at No. 24 Daryaganj Delhi, which has now grown into three institutions – Lala Raghubir Singh Junior Modern School Humayun Road, Modern School Barakhamba Road & Modern School Vasant Vihar – all located in New Delhi. Uniting utility with beauty, the nine-acre campus of Modern School with exposed brickwork, arched openings, circular windows and long corridors lined are a delight every morning for students and teachers. The natural lighting and open spaces are natural pathways for cool fresh air which keep the classrooms well-ventilated throughout the day. The campus affords large spaces for track and field activities, lawn tennis, badminton, basketball, soccer and also has an Olympic size swimming pool.
